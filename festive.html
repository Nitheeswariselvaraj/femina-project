The Blue Brigade

Just as white became 2018's favourite sari hue, blue lehengas seemed to be all the rage with Bollywood this year. While Raw Mango loyalist Neha Dhupia went the regal route at Poorna Patel's wedding in a gold and navy lehenga, Kriti Sanon was unmissable back in May in her royal blue Anita Dongre, covered in fine sky blue threadwork. Giving the traditional lehenga a millennial-approved spin was Aditi Rao Hydari, who skipped on fussy blouses and paired her indigo and ivory Ridhi Mehra skirt with a knotted shirt.

Kriti Sanon is an Indian actress who appears predominantly in Hindi films. Janhvi Kapoor in Manish Malhotra

Janhvi Kapoor is an Indian actress who works in Hindi films. Born to Sridevi and Boney Kapoor, she made her acting debut in 2018 with the romantic drama film Dhadak, which was a commercial success. Kapoor has since starred in the Netflix films Ghost Stories and Gunjan Saxena: The Kargil Girl After bringing in a fashion revolution in Bollywood films in the ’80s, Manish Malhotra decided to turn into a full-fledged couturier in the ’90s. Fast forward to 2019, he has become the go-to costume designer and couturier for cinematic glamour, effortlessly balancing the two worlds of fashion and film. From celebrity brides like Kareena Kapoor Khan, Preity Zinta and Mira Rajput Kapoor, to the onscreen bridal outfits for Anushka Sharma and Alia Bhatt, Malhotra has crafted innumerable wedding creations for both on- and off-screen in the past 27 years. Last year, his flagship boutique in New Delhi was refurbished to a colossal 15,000sqft space, while his two-storied Mumbai outpost is spread over 6,000sqft, making the two couture havens for brides across the country.
